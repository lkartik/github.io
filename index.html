Kartik LakshminarasimhanGraduate Student at UGent

Education

I am currently a PhD Candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at UGent and a member of Performance Lab where I work with Prof. Lieven Eeckhout (Gewoon hoogleraar BOF-ZAP). I have a Master's from Univesity of Connecticut. I graduated with a Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication at SRM Easwari Engineering College, Anna University. During my Bachelors, I spent most of my time with Prof.Waran at WARFT, reading fundamentals and a few research papers.

Research

I work on Complexity-effective general-purpose processor microarchitectures suitable for edge devices. Also, I am interested in characterizing Deep Learning applications. Prior joining UGent, I was a research intern at Intel Microachitecture Research Labs, Bangalore, creating a visualization tool for a CPU's critical path model.
